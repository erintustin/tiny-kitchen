Baked Chicken

Description

Make as much of this simple chicken as you want and use it for your recipes throughout the week. This recipe uses oregano, but you can use different spices depending on what kind of meals you're preparing.

Ingredients

  • thin-sliced chicken breast cutlets
  • salt
  • pepper
  • oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
  3. Place chicken in one layer on parchment paper.
  4. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  5. Bake for 20-30 min.
