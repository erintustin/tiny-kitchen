Pierogi Pot Pie

Description

This easy stovetop recipe pairs classic chicken pot pie filling with potato pierogies for a delicious and filling one-pan meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. package frozen potato and onion pierogies
  • 10 oz. Condensed Cream of Mushroom soup
  • 12 oz. frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 cup milk (or milk substitute)
  • 1/2 lb baked chicken, cubed

Steps

  1. Mix cream of mushroom soup with 1/2 cup milk in a large saucepan on high.
  2. Stir until soup has a smooth, creamy texture, then turn heat to medium
  3. Add frozen pierogies and frozen veggies and cover
  4. Simmer on low until pierogies are cooked (texture is soft and they appear to float).
  5. Add cubed chicken, stir, and serve.
